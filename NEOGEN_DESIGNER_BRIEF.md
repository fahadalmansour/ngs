## Design References & Mood

Look at these stores for inspiration (layout and feel, not copy):

1. **noon.com** - Clean Arabic e-commerce, trust bars, mobile-first
2. **jarir.com** - Arabic electronics store, category navigation
3. **store.google.com** - Clean product pages, minimal design
4. **switchbot.com** - Smart home product presentation
5. **ubiqiti.ui.com** - Premium tech product cards

### Feel
- **Clean** - lots of white space, not cluttered
- **Professional** - not cheap-looking, not over-designed
- **Technical** - specs and compatibility are front-and-center
- **Arabic-native** - not a translated English site, designed for Arabic from scratch
