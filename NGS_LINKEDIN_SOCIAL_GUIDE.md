# PAID ADVERTISING

## Meta Ads (Instagram/Facebook)

### Campaign Types

**1. Awareness Campaigns**
- Objective: Brand awareness, reach
- Audience: Broad, interests-based
- Budget: 20% of ad spend
- Content: Brand videos, educational

**2. Consideration Campaigns**
- Objective: Traffic, engagement
- Audience: Warm audiences, lookalikes
- Budget: 30% of ad spend
- Content: Product demos, reviews

**3. Conversion Campaigns**
- Objective: Purchases, add to cart
- Audience: Retargeting, high-intent
- Budget: 50% of ad spend
- Content: Product ads, offers

### Audience Targeting

**Interest-Based:**
- Smart home
- Home automation
- Internet of Things
- Technology gadgets
- Apple HomeKit / Google Home / Alexa

**Demographic:**
- Age: 25-55
- Location: Saudi Arabia (focus on Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam)
- Language: Arabic
- Income: Medium-high

**Custom Audiences:**
- Website visitors (pixel)
- Instagram engagers
- Customer email list
- Video viewers

**Lookalike Audiences:**
- 1% of purchasers
- 1% of high-value customers
- 1% of email subscribers

### Ad Creative Best Practices

**Image Ads:**
- Product on clean background
- Arabic text overlay (max 20%)
- Price and "Shop Now" CTA
- Before/after comparisons

**Video Ads:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Show product in use
- Square (1:1) for feed
- Vertical (9:16) for Stories/Reels
- Subtitles in Arabic

**Carousel Ads:**
- Feature multiple products
- Step-by-step tutorial
- Customer testimonials
- Product + benefit combination
